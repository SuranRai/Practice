Making Burger With Suran

Ingredients

  • Soft bun
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickle
  • Mayonese
  • Lettuce
  • White Onion

Instruction

  1. First we need to grill the buns for little bit of smokey flavor
  2. Cook your choice of meat, for me I chose beef
  3. Cut the Tomatoes into slice and get cheese from package
  4. Spread the buns and add Mayonese in the bottom bun
  5. After adding mayonese add cooked meat patty
  6. After adding patty add pickle,tomatoes slice, Onion and lettuce
  7. At last add cheese on top of patty and cover it with top bun
